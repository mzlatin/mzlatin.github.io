Michael Zlatin

I am an Assistant Professor of Computer Science at Pomona College. Before that, I was a postdoctoral fellow at Carnegie Mellon University hosted by Professor R. Ravi , and at the Weizmann Institute of Science hosted by Professor Robi Krauthgamer.

I obtained my Ph.D. in Algorithms, Combinatorics and Optimization at the Tepper School of Business, Carnegie Mellon University, where I was fortunate to be advised by Gérard Cornuéjols.
